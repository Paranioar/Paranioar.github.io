About Me

PROFESSIONAL PATH

I am a third-year Ph.D. student supervised by Prof. Huchuan Lu at IIAU-Lab from Dalian University of Technology. I also work in cooperation with Ph.D. Bo Wan from KU Leuven and Asst. Prof. Long Chen from HKUST.
My research interests are in the areas of Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision, particularly in Vision-and-Language, Parameter-efficient Transfer Learning, and Multimodal Large Language Model.
Specifically, my research strives to develop an efficient agent that entails recognizing visual-semantic perception, contextualizing fine-grained correspondence across modalities, and mimicking human-like judgement and decision.

From Sep. 2023, I currently work as a research intern at BAAI with Dr. Xinlong Wang on Parameter-efficient Transfer Learning and Multimodal Large Language Model.
From Jun. 2020 to Mar. 2021, I interned at Tencent AI Lab with Dr. Ying Zhang and Dr. Lin Ma on Image-Text Retrieval, Cross-modal Boosting and Metric Learning.
